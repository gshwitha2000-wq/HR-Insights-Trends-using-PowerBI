# 📊 HR Insights & Trends Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

The **HR Insights & Trends Dashboard** provides a comprehensive analysis of employee data to uncover key workforce patterns, attrition trends, salary distribution, and demographic insights. This dashboard helps HR teams and decision-makers make data-driven strategies for improving employee engagement and organizational performance.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 👥 Workforce Overview

* **Total Employees:** 1480
* **Average Age:** 36.92 years
* **Average Salary:** 6.50K
* **Average Working Years:** 11.28
* **Average Years at Company:** 7.01
* **Daily Rate Average:** 801.38

---

### 📉 Attrition Analysis

* Gender-based attrition comparison (Male vs Female)
* Job role-wise attrition breakdown
* Helps identify high-risk employee segments

---

### 📊 Salary & Compensation Insights

* Daily rate analysis across:

  * Salary slabs
  * Job roles
  * Age groups
* Identifies compensation trends and disparities

### 🎓 Education Insights

* Distribution of employees across education fields:

  * Life Sciences (highest)
  * Medical
  * Marketing
  * Technical Degree
* Department-wise education demographics

### 📈 Trends & Patterns

* Daily rate trends across years at the company
* Age group analysis:

  * Highest contribution from 26–35 age group

### 🧑‍💼 Job Role Insights

## 📈 Key Insights

* Majority of employees belong to the **26–35 age group**.
* **Life Sciences** dominates in education background.
* Employees with lower salary slabs contribute more to daily rate totals.
* Attrition varies significantly across job roles and genders.
* Experience (years at company) has a visible impact on compensation trends.

## ▶️ How to Use

1. Open the dashboard in Power BI / Tableau.
2. Use filters (Department: HR, R&D, Sales) to explore specific data.
3. Hover over visuals for detailed insights.
4. Analyze patterns to support HR decision-making.
